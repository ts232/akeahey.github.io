Premier League Table Race Map

This project, inspired by some of my favourite formula one race visualizations, will create a "racemap" of the premier league season based on standings each week.

Already, the steady standing of Liverpool at first position, the fall of Tottenham, the surprising rise of newly promoted Sheffield United, and the chaos of week 12 are creating interesting questions:

Compared to other seasons, how typical is this year's pattern of movement on the table?

  • After which week are the relative positions of teams mostly set?

    • Also hoping to create an interactive tool to compare seasons with d3.js
